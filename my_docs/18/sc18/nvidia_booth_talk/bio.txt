Dr Robert McLay received his Ph.D. from the University of Texas at
Austin in Engineering Mechanics.  His interests include 3D Parallel
Finite Element simulation and tools that aid parallel
computations. Since 2008, he has worked at the Texas Advance Computing
Center (TACC) where he now leads the Software Tools group.  He is also
the chief developer of Lmod, a modern environment module tool and XALT
a tool to track what programs and libraries run on a cluster.
